Title: The Wand and the Memory
Logline:
Over a lifetime, Harry Potter and Hermione Granger are bound by a single wand, whose fading magic mirrors the passage of time and the endurance of friendship.
Synopsis:
In the Hogwarts Library, young Harry (20) and Hermione (20) discover that Harry’s wand (polished oak, faintly glowing) holds echoes of their past courage. Decades later, in the misty Forbidden Forest, middle-aged Harry (50) and Hermione (50) reunite, following the wand’s dim light to the scarred tree where they once stood together. In their old cottage, elderly Harry (80) gently polishes the wand (splintered, barely glowing) as Hermione (80) brings tea; when the wand flickers one last golden light, they smile — realizing that true magic was never in the spell, but in their shared memories.
