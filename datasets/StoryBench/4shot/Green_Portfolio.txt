Title: Green Portfolio

Shot1: On the windswept observation deck of a skyscraper, CHLOE (20s, dressed in a stylish trench coat, her hair blowing in the wind) looks through a pair of tourist binoculars. Leaning against the railing next to her is a large, neon-green artist's portfolio (a rigid case, at least two feet wide, with a shockingly bright color that contrasts with the grey cityscape).

Shot2: The neon-green portfolio lies on a drafting table in a minimalist, white-walled art studio. A single, perfect architectural drawing is clipped to the table beside it.

Shot3: Chloe is now in a vast, empty warehouse space. The room is dusty and filled with echoes. She paces the concrete floor, visualizing, her arms outstretched as if measuring the space.

Shot4: Back in the art studio, Chloe stands before a large, newly started canvas. It's a painting of the warehouse, but transformed with incredible light and color. She reaches for a brush, her neon-green portfolio open on the floor beside her, filled with inspirational sketches.
