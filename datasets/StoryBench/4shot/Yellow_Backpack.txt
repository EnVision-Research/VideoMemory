Title: Yellow Backpack

Shot1: In a quiet urban park at dawn, ELARA (30s, dressed in comfortable running gear) stretches against a tree. Beside her on the grass rests a large, bright-yellow backpack (a modern, technical hiking pack, vividly colored and big enough for a multi-day trek), which seems out of place in the city setting. She has a look of calm determination.

Shot2: The bright-yellow backpack sits alone on a bench at a deserted rural bus stop. A timetable on the shelter wall flaps in the gentle breeze. The sun is higher in the sky now, illuminating a dusty country road that stretches into the distance.

Shot3: Elara (now looking tired and dusty, a smudge of dirt on her cheek) consults a small, traditional paper map at a crossroads in the middle of a forest. The path ahead splits in two, and she seems momentarily uncertain.

Shot4: At the summit of a small mountain, the view of the valley below is breathtaking. Elara sits on a rock, taking a long drink from a water bottle. Her bright-yellow backpack is on the ground next to her, a symbol of the journey completed. She smiles, serene and accomplished.
