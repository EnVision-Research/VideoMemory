Title: Blue Thermos

Shot1: In a quiet, sunlit library, ANNA (20s, a student wearing headphones and a university sweatshirt) is deeply focused on her laptop. Next to her on the large oak table is a vibrant-blue thermos (an unusually large, flask-style container, easily big enough to hold a liter of coffee, in a brilliant cobalt blue).

Shot2: The vibrant-blue thermos sits on the edge of a fountain in a bustling university courtyard. Students walk past, chatting and laughing, but the thermos remains untouched.

Shot3: Anna is inside a hushed, empty lecture hall, searching under the tiered rows of seats. She looks increasingly worried, retracing her steps from earlier in the day.

Shot4: Anna is back in the library, looking defeated. A kindly librarian approaches her table, holding the vibrant-blue thermos. Anna's face breaks into a wide, relieved smile as she takes it back, clutching it thankfully.
