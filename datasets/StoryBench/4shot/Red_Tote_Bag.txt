Title: Red Tote Bag

Shot1: In a small, independent bookstore, ISLA (60s, with sharp, intelligent eyes and a colourful scarf) pulls a book from a high shelf. Leaning against the bookshelf is her large, bright-red tote bag (a canvas bag, large and sturdy enough to hold a dozen books, with a bold crimson hue).

Shot2: The bright-red tote bag, now visibly full and heavy, rests on a table at a cozy corner café. A half-empty cup of tea sits beside it.

Shot3: Isla is walking along a riverbank, hands tucked in her pockets, watching a ferry pass by. She seems to be lost in thought, puzzling over something.

Shot4: Isla sits on a park bench, the bright-red tote bag beside her. She has taken out one of the new books and a notebook. A pen flies across the page as inspiration strikes; she is writing furiously, a look of intense concentration and breakthrough on her face.
