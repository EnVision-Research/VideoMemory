Title: Red Rocking Horse

Shot1: In a cluttered but cozy workshop, LEO (40s, with kind eyes and wearing a paint-splattered apron) proudly holds up a newly finished piece of furniture: a large, bright-red wooden rocking horse (expertly carved and painted a brilliant, high-gloss crimson, with a flowing white mane).

Shot2: The bright-red rocking horse sits in the back of an open-bed pickup truck, which is driving down a suburban street lined with neat houses and green lawns.

Shot3: Leo stands on the front porch of a small house, ringing the doorbell. He looks down at his hands, a flicker of nervous anticipation on his face. The door remains closed.

Shot4: Inside a child's brightly decorated bedroom, a little girl is laughing with delight as she rides the horse. Leo kneels beside her, his hand resting on the bright-red rocking horse, watching her with a smile filled with warmth and love.
