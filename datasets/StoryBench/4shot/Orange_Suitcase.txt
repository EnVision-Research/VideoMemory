Title: Orange Suitcase

Shot1: In a grand, historic train station, MARK (50s, a professorly type in a tweed jacket with elbow patches) checks his watch while holding a large, fluorescent-orange suitcase (a hard-shell case with a very modern, almost glowing color that stands out against the station's old-world architecture).

Shot2: The fluorescent-orange suitcase glides along a luggage carousel at a small, regional airport. It's one of the last bags to emerge.

Shot3: Mark is driving a vintage convertible down a scenic coastal highway. The sun is setting, and the sea air whips through his hair. He looks relaxed and free.

Shot4: On a sandy beach, a small group of students is gathered around a bonfire. Mark opens the fluorescent-orange suitcase, but instead of clothes, it's full of old books. He pulls one out and begins to read aloud to the captivated group as the flames dance.
