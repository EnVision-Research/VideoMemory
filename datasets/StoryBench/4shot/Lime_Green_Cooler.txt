Title: Lime Green Cooler

Shot1: At a bustling outdoor farmers market, JAVIER (20s, a cheerful chef in a white coat) inspects a crate of fresh tomatoes. He carries a large, lime-green cooler box (a big, retro-style cooler, its vibrant color making it pop amongst the earthy tones of the market produce).

Shot2: The lime-green cooler box sits on the stainless-steel counter of a pristine, professional kitchen. The lid is open, revealing it to be empty.

Shot3: Javier is now in a beautifully manicured herb garden, carefully snipping fresh basil and rosemary, placing the cuttings into a small basket.

Shot4: Back in the kitchen, Javier places the last of the beautiful vegetables and herbs he has gathered into the lime-green cooler box. He closes the lid with a satisfied nod, ready to transport his fresh ingredients for the evening's special dinner service.
