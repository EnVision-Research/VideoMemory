Title: Blue Vase

Shot1: In a cramped apartment filled with moving boxes, MAYA (30s, looking weary but hopeful) carefully wraps a large, electric-blue ceramic vase (a handcrafted, artistic piece, the size of a small table lamp, with a stunning, iridescent glaze) in bubble wrap.

Shot2: The electric-blue vase sits alone on the polished wooden floor in the center of a completely empty, sun-drenched room in a new house.

Shot3: Maya is outside in the new home's slightly overgrown backyard. She's sketching in a notepad, planning where flowers and a small garden will go.

Shot4: The living room is now partially furnished, starting to feel like home. Maya places a bouquet of freshly picked wildflowers from her new garden into the electric-blue vase, which now sits as the centerpiece on a small coffee table. She steps back and smiles, content.
