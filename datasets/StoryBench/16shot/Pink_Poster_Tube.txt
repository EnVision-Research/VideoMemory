Title: Pink Poster Tube

Shot1: ANNA (25, in a simple dress, looking nervous) stands in her cluttered apartment, holding a LARGE NEON-PINK POSTER TUBE (a bright, sturdy cardboard cylinder).

Shot2: She pulls a small, hand-drawn map from the tube. It shows a route to an unknown location marked with a star.

Shot3: Anna looks at the map, then at her front door, and takes a deep breath.

Shot4: She is on a crowded city bus, holding the map and the tube, looking out the window as the familiar neighborhood changes.

Shot5: Anna gets off the bus in an industrial area she doesn't recognize. The streets are mostly empty.

Shot6: She consults the map, turns, and walks down a narrow alleyway between two brick buildings.

Shot7: The alley is dark, and her steps echo. She looks nervous and almost turns back.

Shot8: At the end of the alley is a single, unmarked metal door.

Shot9: She hesitates for a long moment, gripping the pink tube tightly.

Shot10: She raises her hand and knocks on the door. A small sliding panel opens, and a pair of eyes look out.

Shot11: The door creaks open, revealing a warm, brightly lit room filled with people and music.

Shot12: Anna steps inside, looking around in wonder. This is the "underground" open-mic venue she was looking for.

Shot13: A person on a small stage finishes a song to applause. The host looks for the next performer.

Shot14: Anna (now with a look of determination) walks toward the stage.

Shot15: She stands on the stage, her map peeking out of the poster tube. She looks at the crowd, her fear replaced with excitement.

Shot16: Anna smiles at the audience, one hand on the microphone and the other still holding her large neon-pink poster tube.
