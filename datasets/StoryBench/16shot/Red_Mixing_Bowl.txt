Title: Red Mixing Bowl

Shot1: SOFIA (30, with flour on her cheek and wearing a baker's apron) stands in her bakery kitchen, holding a LARGE, BRIGHT RED MIXING BOWL (a heavy ceramic bowl).

Shot2: She is trying to recreate her grandmother's famous bread. She looks at a faded, handwritten recipe card.

Shot3: One key ingredient on the card is smudged and unreadable.

Shot4: She mixes the dough, making a guess. She puts the loaf in the oven.

Shot5: The timer dings. She pulls out the bread. It's flat, dense, and dark. She sighs and throws it away.

Shot6: She tries again, guessing a different ingredient.

Shot7: This loaf also comes out wrong, pale and crumbly. She looks completely frustrated.

Shot8: Sofia leaves the kitchen, taking off her apron.

Shot9: She is now in a small, dusty attic, opening an old wooden trunk.

Shot10: She pulls out a stack of old letters tied with a ribbon.

Shot11: She sits on the floor and reads a letter from her grandmother. It describes the bakery.

Shot12: The letter mentions her "secret," a spoonful of honey "for the golden color."

Shot13: Sofia's eyes widen. She runs back to the bakery kitchen.

Shot14: She mixes a new batch of dough, adding a spoonful of honey. The dough looks perfect.

Shot15: The oven timer dings. She pulls out a beautifully risen, golden-brown loaf. She smiles, tearing up.

Shot16: Sofia places the perfect loaf on a cooling rack, next to the large, bright red mixing bowl.
