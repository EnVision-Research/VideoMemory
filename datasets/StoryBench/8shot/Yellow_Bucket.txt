Title: Yellow Bucket

Shot1: CHLOE (24, with her pants rolled up, wearing a light blue windbreaker) walks along a sandy shoreline at low tide, carrying a BRIGHT YELLOW BUCKET (a large plastic pail).

Shot2: She bends down and picks up a piece of a broken seashell, inspects it, and tosses it back into the surf with a sigh.

Shot3: The yellow bucket sits on the sand next to her. It is completely empty.

Shot4: Chloe walks away from the main sandy beach and climbs over some dark, wet rocks to a series of tide pools.

Shot5: She peers into a shallow pool, brushing aside some dark green seaweed to see the bottom.

Shot6: She spots something half-buried in the sand at the bottom of the pool. She reaches her arm deep into the cold water.

Shot7: Chloe gently lifts a large, perfect orange starfish from the water and smiles as she admires it.

Shot8: She carefully places the starfish into her bright yellow bucket, which now also contains a bit of seawater, and continues her walk.
