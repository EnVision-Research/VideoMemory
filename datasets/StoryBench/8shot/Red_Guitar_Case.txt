Title: Red Guitar Case

Shot1: SAM (22, in a faded t-shirt) sits on his apartment floor, guitar in hand, next to his open, BRIGHT RED GUITAR CASE (lined with black velvet). He strums a chord and sighs.

Shot2: He scribbles notes on a piece of paper, crosses them out, and crumples the paper, throwing it onto a pile of other crumpled papers.

Shot3: Sam is now standing in a busy subway station. The noise of trains screeching and people talking is overwhelming. He winces.

Shot4: He walks out of the station and into a quiet, tree-lined park. He sits on a bench, closing his eyes, just listening.

Shot5: A bird sings a distinct, cheerful melody from a nearby branch. Sam's eyes open, and he pulls a small notebook from his pocket.

Shot6: He begins to hum, tapping a rhythm on his knee. He smiles as he writes a few notes in his book.

Shot7: His guitar is out of its case. Sam sits on the park bench, playing a gentle, flowing melody that sounds happy and complete.

Shot8: Sam places his guitar back into the bright red guitar case, zips it up, and walks away with a peaceful expression.
