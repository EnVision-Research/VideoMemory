Title: Green Recipe Box

Shot1: LEO (26, wearing a clean white chef's coat) blows dust off a BRIGHT GREEN RECIPE BOX (an old, painted tin box) in his sleek, modern kitchen.

Shot2: He pulls out a faded, handwritten recipe card. He squints, trying to read an ingredient that has been smudged by water.

Shot3: Leo types the recipe name into his laptop. The search results show modern versions, but not this specific old one.

Shot4: He is now inside a public library, walking through the cookbook aisle, looking at the spines.

Shot5: He pulls a very thick, dusty regional cookbook from the 1950s off the shelf and takes it to a table.

Shot6: He flips through the pages. He finds a similar recipe and his eyes light up as he identifies the "mystery" ingredient as star anise.

Shot7: Back in his kitchen, Leo (coat off, sleeves rolled up) adds a star anise pod to a simmering pot, which smells delicious.

Shot8: Leo smiles and closes the lid of the bright green recipe box, which sits on the counter next to a steaming bowl of stew.
