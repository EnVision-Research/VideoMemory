Title: Pink Box

Shot1: JIN (30, wearing a blue company uniform) scans a label on a LARGE, NEON-PINK BOX (a brightly colored, cube-shaped shipping container) in a busy mailroom.

Shot2: He drives his small delivery van down a residential street, looking at house numbers in the fading afternoon light.

Shot3: Jin gets out of the van and stands in front of an empty, overgrown lot. He looks at the address on his scanner, then at the lot, confused.

Shot4: He looks closely at the shipping label on the neon-pink box. He sees the street name is "Elm *Avenue*," not "Elm *Street*."

Shot5: Back in his van, Jin drives through a different, older neighborhood with large trees.

Shot6: He pulls up to a house with "120" clearly on the mailbox, matching the address for "Elm Avenue."

Shot7: A person opens the door and breaks into a huge smile upon seeing the package.

Shot8: Jin hands the large, neon-pink box to the smiling person, tips his cap, and walks back to his van.
