Title: Blue Kite

Shot1: ARTHUR (50, with graying hair and a gentle expression) ties the final knot on the tail of a LARGE, BRIGHT BLUE KITE (a complex box kite design) in his cluttered workshop.

Shot2: He stands in the middle of a wide, grassy park. The air is completely still. He holds the kite up, but it doesn't catch any wind.

Shot3: Arthur sits on a park bench, the kite resting beside him. He watches the leaves on a tree, waiting for them to move.

Shot4: A sudden, strong gust of wind blows, knocking over a nearby trash can. Arthur struggles to hold onto the kite as it's whipped around.

Shot5: He walks away from the open field, heading toward a small, rounded hill at the edge of the park.

Shot6: Now on top of the hill, the wind seems steadier and gentler.

Shot7: The bright blue kite soars high in the sky, stable and graceful against the white clouds.

Shot8: Arthur stands on the hill, holding the string, looking up at his large, bright blue kite with a proud smile.
