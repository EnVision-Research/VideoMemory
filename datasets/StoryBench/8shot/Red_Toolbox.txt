Title: Red Toolbox

Shot1: RYAN (32, in a comfortable sweater) sips coffee and looks out his apartment window at the pouring rain. His gaze lands on a LARGE, BRIGHT-RED TOOLBOX (a classic, metal cantilever box) in the corner.

Shot2: He opens the toolbox. Inside, instead of tools, are bags of plastic bricks, instructions, and a baseplate for a building set.

Shot3: Ryan sits at his dining room table, carefully following the instructions and snapping bricks together. A small house begins to take shape.

Shot4: He consults the instruction sheet, then frantically searches through the box and the piles of bricks on the table. He can't find the small red door.

Shot5: He gets on his hands and knees, searching the floor under the table among the dust bunnies.

Shot6: Ryan looks on a nearby bookshelf and finds the small red door piece, which must have fallen earlier, resting next to a photo frame.

Shot7: The completed brick house, red door attached, sits proudly on the table. The rain has softened to a light drizzle.

Shot8: Ryan (smiling) closes the lid of the bright-red toolbox, placing the finished house on top of it.
