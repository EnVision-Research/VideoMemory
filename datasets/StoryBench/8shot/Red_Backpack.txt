Title: Red Backpack

Shot1: MARK (28, wearing a durable green hiking jacket and cap) adjusts the straps of his BRIGHT RED BACKPACK (a large, sturdy hiking pack that stands out) at a sunny trailhead.

Shot2: Mark walks confidently along a narrow dirt path, surrounded by tall pine trees. The sun filters through the canopy above.

Shot3: The path forks. Mark pauses, checks a small paper map, and confidently chooses the left path, which quickly begins to look overgrown.

Shot4: He scrambles down a steep, muddy slope, slipping on loose gravel. His bright red backpack tumbles away from him, rolling down the hill.

Shot5: At the bottom of the slope, Mark (now with mud stains on his jacket) looks around frantically in the thick undergrowth. The backpack is gone.

Shot6: He pushes through dense bushes, searching. The light is dimmer here, and the sounds of the main trail are gone.

Shot7: The bright red backpack is visible, snagged on a fallen log near a small, gurgling stream, just a few feet from where he is standing.

Shot8: Now on a different, wider path, Mark (looking relieved) zips a side pocket on the bright red backpack and continues his hike with a smile.
