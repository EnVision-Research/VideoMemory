Title: Green Canvas

Shot1: ELARA (35, with paint splatters on her apron and tired eyes) stands in her bright, messy studio, staring at a LARGE FLUORESCENT GREEN CANVAS (a stark, empty 4x4 foot square).

Shot2: She dabs a brush with blue paint, touches it to the canvas, and immediately wipes it off with a rag, frustrated.

Shot3: Elara is now walking through a bustling city park, away from her studio. She sits on a bench, watching people and pigeons.

Shot4: Her gaze follows a child blowing bubbles. A large, iridescent bubble floats past her face and pops against a tree.

Shot5: She looks up at the city buildings, noticing the geometric patterns of the windows reflecting the sunset. A small smile forms.

Shot6: Back in her studio, Elara (no longer wearing the apron) stands before the canvas, holding a brush, and takes a deep breath.

Shot7: The large fluorescent green canvas is no longer empty. It is now covered in sweeping, energetic circles and bold blue lines.

Shot8: Elara stands back from the large fluorescent green canvas, her brush in hand, a look of deep satisfaction on her face.
