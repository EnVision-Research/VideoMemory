Title: Yellow Book

Shot1: LIAM (42, wearing glasses and a tweed vest) pulls a very large, BRIGHT YELLOW BOOK (a heavy, leather-bound volume) from a dusty shelf in a vast, quiet library.

Shot2: He sits at a wooden table, carefully turning the old, illustrated pages. He stops on a page with a detailed drawing of a constellation.

Shot3: Liam walks to a different section of the library, a circular room with a domed ceiling painted like the night sky.

Shot4: He looks up at the painted ceiling, comparing it to the illustration he saw in the book. He notices one painted star seems different.

Shot5: He finds a rolling ladder and carefully climbs up. He reaches out and touches the "star," which is actually a small, embedded brass button.

Shot6: Liam presses the button. A nearby section of the bookshelf clicks and slides open, revealing a small, hidden compartment.

Shot7: Inside the compartment is a small, rolled-up parchment map tied with a ribbon.

Shot8: Liam sits back at the table, the parchment map spread open next to the bright yellow book, and he studies them both intently.
