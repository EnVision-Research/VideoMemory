Title: Pink Sculpture

Shot1: Inside a sterile, white-walled art gallery, MAYA (20s, an aspiring artist, dressed in black, looking nervous) makes a final adjustment to her artwork. It is a large, fluorescent-pink sculpture (an abstract piece made of twisted metal, painted an intensely vibrant, almost glowing pink).

Shot2: The fluorescent-pink sculpture sits alone in the gallery space after closing hours. The stark white walls and polished concrete floor make its color seem even more intense.

Shot3: Maya is at home, unable to sleep. She scrolls through social media, looking at comments about the new art exhibition. Her expression is a mixture of hope and anxiety.

Shot4: The next day, she walks back towards the gallery. She hesitates outside the glass doors, afraid to go in and see the public's reaction.

Shot5: She sees a famous, intimidatingly stern art critic enter the gallery. Maya's heart sinks.

Shot6: From the street, she watches the critic walk slowly through the exhibition. He stops for a very long time in front of her sculpture. He has an unreadable, poker-faced expression.

Shot7: The critic takes out a small notebook and jots something down before moving on. Maya can't bear the suspense and walks away.

Shot8: She sits on a park bench, feeding pigeons, trying to distract herself. She looks utterly dejected, convinced she has failed.

Shot9: Her phone buzzes. It's a message from the gallery owner. It just says, "You need to see this. Come back."

Shot10: Maya re-enters the gallery. A small crowd is gathered around her piece. They aren't laughing; they are looking at it with genuine interest and admiration.

Shot11: The gallery owner shows Maya a copy of the critic's online review. The headline is glowing, calling her work "A bold and triumphant debut."

Shot12: Maya stands beside her fluorescent-pink sculpture, no longer nervous but with a newfound confidence. A small "SOLD" sticker is now discreetly placed on the title card next to her art.
