Title: Lime Green Duffel Bag

Shot1: At the base of a towering, ice-covered mountain, KAI (30s, a rugged and experienced climber, dressed in full arctic gear) secures a large, lime-green duffel bag (a heavy-duty, waterproof expedition bag, its fluorescent color designed for high visibility in the snow) to his harness.

Shot2: The lime-green bag is shown halfway up a sheer ice wall, dangling from a rope anchor point. It is the only point of color in a world of white and blue.

Shot3: Kai is making his way up the ice wall, using ice axes and crampons. The climb is physically grueling and requires intense concentration.

Shot4: A sudden, fierce blizzard blows in, dramatically reducing visibility. The wind howls around him.

Shot5: Kai loses his footing for a terrifying moment but manages to arrest his fall. He is shaken, clinging to the side of the mountain.

Shot6: He finds a small ice cave, a tiny hollow in the side of the mountain, and scrambles inside for shelter. He is shivering violently.

Shot7: He manages to haul his duffel bag into the cave with him. He fumbles with the frozen zipper, his fingers numb.

Shot8: Inside the bag is a high-altitude tent, a sleeping bag, and a small stove. He works quickly, his survival instincts taking over.

Shot9: He gets the small stove lit. The tiny flame provides a small amount of warmth and light inside the dark ice cave. He huddles close to it.

Shot10: Hours later, the blizzard has passed. The world outside the cave is silent and covered in a fresh layer of snow under a clear, starry sky.

Shot11: Kai emerges from the cave. He looks exhausted but alive. He gazes up at the mountain peak, which is now visible and seems closer than ever.

Shot12: At the summit of the mountain, Kai stands triumphantly against a backdrop of a stunning sunrise. His lime-green duffel bag is at his feet, a silent testament to the journey and the tool that helped him survive it.
