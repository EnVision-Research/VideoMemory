Title: Fake Scarlet Rose

Shot1: An empty community theater stage. MIKE (45, in casual clothes) paces back and forth, reciting lines to himself.

Shot2: A dim backstage hallway. Mike leans against a wall, looking nervous.

Shot3: A crowded costume shop. Mike tries on a large, feathered hat.

Shot4: A public park. Mike sits on a bench, reading his script, mouthing the words.

Shot5: A small, messy dressing room. Mike sits at a mirror, applying stage makeup.

Shot6: A props table just off-stage. Mike picks up a single, FAKE SCARLET ROSE (A large, obviously artificial rose made of bright red silk).

Shot7: The wings of the stage. Mike holds the fake scarlet rose, takes a deep breath, and waits for his cue.

Shot8: A coffee shop, much later. Mike sits with friends, laughing, the fake scarlet rose resting on the table in front of him.
