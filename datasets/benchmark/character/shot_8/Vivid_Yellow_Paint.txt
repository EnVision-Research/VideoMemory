Title: Vivid Yellow Paint

Shot1: A cluttered artist's studio. LEO (40, in a paint-splattered apron) stretches a large, blank canvas.

Shot2: A local art supply store. Leo compares two different tubes of blue paint.

Shot3: A city sidewalk. Leo walks briskly, carrying his new supplies.

Shot4: A public park. Leo sits on the grass, sketching ideas in a notepad.

Shot5: A museum gallery. Leo stands motionless, studying a large abstract painting.

Shot6: A hardware store. Leo has a clerk mix a custom, VIVID YELLOW PAINT (A large, 1-gallon can of high-gloss, lemon-yellow paint).

Shot7: A quiet alleyway. Leo leans against a brick wall, catching his breath and holding the can of vivid yellow paint.

Shot8: The artist's studio. Leo is in front of the canvas, which is now entirely coated in the vivid yellow paint, ready for him to begin.
