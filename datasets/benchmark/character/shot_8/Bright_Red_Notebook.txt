Title: Bright Red Notebook

Shot1: A bright, modern kitchen. ANNA (28, in a cozy sweater and jeans) stares at a blank sheet of music on the counter, looking frustrated.

Shot2: A quiet home office. Anna sits at a desk, tapping a pencil, still unable to write.

Shot3: A local music store. Anna browses through aisles of instruments, looking for inspiration.

Shot4: A small, crowded cafe. Anna sips a coffee, still looking thoughtful.

Shot5: A park bench. Anna pulls out a LARGE, BRIGHT RED NOTEBOOK (A thick, leather-bound journal, A4 size) and a pen.

Shot6: A moving city bus. Anna scribbles furiously in the bright red notebook, a melody clearly striking her as she looks out the window.

Shot7: A building elevator. Anna taps her fingers on the cover of the bright red notebook, a small smile on her face.

Shot8: The home office again. Anna sits at her piano, the open bright red notebook in front of her, playing a beautiful new melody.
