Title: Vivid Red Umbrella

Shot1: A misty forest trail at dawn. KENJI (42, in full hiking gear) checks his compass, looking slightly lost.

Shot2: A rocky riverbed. Kenji carefully steps from stone to stone, balancing.

Shot3: A wide, grassy meadow. Kenji stops, looking exhausted, and takes a VIVID RED UMBRELLA (A large, sturdy golf umbrella) from his pack.

Shot4: A small campsite by a lake. Kenji sits by a tent, the vivid red umbrella now open and stuck in the ground next to him, shielding his map from a light drizzle.
