Title: Vivid Red Backpack

Shot1: In a quiet, wood-paneled library, MARK (42, in a comfortable sweater) scans a bookshelf.

Shot2: Still in the library, Mark pulls out a very old, large book and blows dust off the cover.

Shot3: In a university archive room, Mark, wearing white gloves, examines an old map.

Shot4: In a local history museum, Mark studies a diorama and takes notes.

Shot5: In a small-town diner, Mark sips coffee, comparing his notes to the old map.

Shot6: In a gravel parking lot, Mark steps out of his car, looking toward the woods.

Shot7: On a forest trail, Mark walks, consulting his notes.

Shot8: In a clearing, Mark stops, looking around, confused.

Shot9: In the clearing, Mark drops his vivid red backpack (a large, technical hiking pack) in frustration.

Shot10: In the clearing, he sits on a log, pulls a granola bar from the vivid red backpack, and eats while studying the map.

Shot11: In the clearing, Mark’s eyes light up as he spots a small, overgrown stone marker.

Shot12: In the clearing, smiling, Mark pulls a camera from the vivid red backpack to photograph his discovery.
