Title: Bright Red Watering Can

Shot1: A small, fenced-off community garden plot. The plot is empty, save for a few weeds.

Shot2: The garden plot. SAM (40s, in gardening gloves and overalls) arrives, carrying a tray of small seedlings.

Shot3: The garden plot. Sam is gone. The seedlings are now planted in neat rows.

Shot4: The garden plot. Sam returns, carrying a BRIGHT RED WATERING CAN (A large, 2-gallon metal can), and begins watering the seedlings.

Shot5: The garden plot. Sam is gone. The bright red watering can rests near the planted rows. The sun is high.

Shot6: The garden plot. Sam is back, pulling weeds from around the small, growing plants. The watering can is not visible.

Shot7: The garden plot. The plants are much larger now, and Sam is gone. The bright red watering can is visible again, sitting by the fence.

Shot8: The garden plot. Sam kneels, harvesting a ripe tomato, smiling. The bright red watering can is next to him.
