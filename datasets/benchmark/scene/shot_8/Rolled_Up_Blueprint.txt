Title: Rolled-Up Blueprint

Shot3: The subway bench. The blueprint is gone. AN ELDERLY MAN (70, with a cane) sits on the bench, reading a newspaper.

Shot4: The subway bench. The elderly man is gone.

Shot5: The subway bench. A YOUNG WOMAN (25, in a business suit) is sitting on the bench, looking at her phone.

Shot7: The subway bench. The YOUNG MAN (28, an architect, looking frantic) runs up, spots the rolled-up blueprint, and snatches it.

Shot8: The subway bench. The young man sits, holding the rolled-up blueprint tightly, checking his watch as he waits for the train.
