Title: Rolled Up Blueprint

Shot1: On a tidy home office desk, only a computer is present.

Shot2: On the desk, a rolled-up blueprint (held with a rubber band, bright cyan outer sheet) is placed.

Shot3: On the desk, the blueprint is gone; MR. HENDERSON (50s, in a dress shirt) sits typing.

Shot4: On the desk, Mr. Henderson is on the phone, looking annoyed.

Shot5: On the desk, Mr. Henderson is gone.

Shot6: On the desk, the rolled-up blueprint has returned, lying in the middle.

Shot7: On the desk, Mr. Henderson comes back and unrolls the blueprint, spreading it across the desk.

Shot8: On the desk, Mr. Henderson studies the blueprint, making notes.

Shot9: On the desk, Mr. Henderson is gone; the blueprint remains open.

Shot10: At the desk, a younger colleague stands beside Mr. Henderson as he points to a detail on the blueprint.

Shot11: On the desk, both are gone; the blueprint is rolled up again.

Shot12: At the desk, Mr. Henderson takes the rolled-up blueprint and leaves the room.
