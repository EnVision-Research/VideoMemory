Title: Neon Pink Canvas

Shot1: An art studio. An empty easel stands in the center of the room.

Shot2: The art studio. MAYA (35, in a paint-splattered apron) enters the room carrying a large, NEON PINK CANVAS (A 3x3 foot canvas, pre-painted a flat, shocking pink).

Shot3: The art studio. Maya stands back, studying the neon pink canvas, which is now on the easel. She is alone.

Shot4: The art studio. Maya is gone. The neon pink canvas is still on the easel, but now has the beginning of a charcoal sketch on it.
