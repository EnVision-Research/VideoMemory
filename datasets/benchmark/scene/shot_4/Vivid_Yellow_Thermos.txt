Title: Vivid Yellow Thermos

Shot1: A quiet park bench at sunrise. A large, VIVID YELLOW THERMOS (A classic steel thermos, painted lemon yellow) sits on the bench.

Shot2: The park bench. ELIZA (60s, in a warm coat) sits beside the vivid yellow thermos, pouring steam into a cup.

Shot3: The park bench. Eliza sips from her cup, reading a newspaper. The thermos is no longer on the bench (presumably at her feet).

Shot4: The park bench. Eliza screws the lid back onto the vivid yellow thermos, packs her things, and stands up to leave.
