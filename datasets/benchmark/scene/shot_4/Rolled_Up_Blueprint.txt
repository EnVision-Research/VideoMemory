Title: Rolled-Up Blueprint

Shot1: A small, tidy office. A large, ROLLED-UP BLUEPRINT (Held together with rubber bands, its outer sheet is bright cyan) rests on the corner of a desk.

Shot2: The office. The blueprint is gone. MR. HENDERSON (50s, in a dress shirt) sits at the desk, typing on his computer.

Shot3: The office. Mr. Henderson is gone. The rolled-up blueprint is now spread open across the desk, revealing a complex architectural design.

Shot4: The office. Mr. Henderson stands over the open blueprint, pointing to a detail on it, explaining it to someone just out of frame.
