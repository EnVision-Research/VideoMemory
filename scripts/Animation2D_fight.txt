TITLE: “EIGHT-COUNT SKY” (global_visual_style: 2D Anime)

ACT 1
1. INT. UNDERGROUND DOJO – NIGHT
Neon kanji signs hum. A circular sparring ring. KAI (22) tightens his gloves, breath visible in cool air.
Across the ring, RIN (24) rolls her shoulders—calm, precise.
RIN
You’re late.
KAI
I’m here now.
2. EXT. DOJO CORRIDOR – CONTINUOUS
A bell rings. The corridor lights flicker like a countdown. Kai steps through sliding doors—focus sharpening.
ACT 2
3. INT. DOJO RING – NIGHT
A digital timer: 00:08.
Rin darts in—clean footwork, no contact yet, testing distance. Kai blocks air with forearms, reading rhythm.
KAI
(smiles)
Eight seconds?
RIN
Eight counts to prove you belong.
4. INT. DOJO RING – CONTINUOUS
They clash—impact flashes and speed lines. Kai pivots, Rin slips past—her palm stops one inch from his chest.
A shockwave ripples the ropes—stylized, nonviolent.
5. INT. DOJO RING – CONTINUOUS
Kai shuts his eyes—listens. On the next beat, he moves first: a flowing combo of feints, Rin counters with a spin-step.
RIN
Good. Again.
ACT 3
6. INT. DOJO RING – CONTINUOUS
Timer hits 00:01. Kai lunges—then stops. He chooses control over force.
Rin’s strike also halts mid-air. Both freeze—mutual respect.
7. INT. DOJO RING – NIGHT
The bell. Silence. Rin tosses him a towel.
RIN
You passed.
Not because you hit…
because you didn’t.
KAI
(grinning)
Next time—nine counts.
8. EXT. DOJO ENTRANCE – NIGHT
They step into rain-slick neon streets. The city reflects like a comic panel.
Kai and Rin walk side by side—rivals, now partners.
FADE OUT.
