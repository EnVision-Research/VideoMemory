**TITLE: THE EDGE OF FOCUS**

**GENRE: 2D Animated Action / Drama**

**LOGLINE:** Two rivals clash in a high-intensity sparring match where their focus warps reality around them.

**KEY PROP:** A bamboo Kendo practice sword (Shinai).

***

**SCENE 1**

**INT. TRADITIONAL DOJO - LATER AFTERNOON**

The animation style is gritty but fluid. Dust motes dance in shafts of golden sunlight cutting through the dark wooden hall.

Close up on a well-worn BAMBOO SHINAI (PROP). A hand grips the leather handle tightly. Knuckles white.

KENJI (20s, sharp features, intense eyes beneath a Kendo helmet grill) stands motionless in a low stance.

Across the polished floor, RYU (20s, taller, stoic expression visible through his helmet grill) mirrors the stance. He also holds a SHINAI (PROP).

The air is heavy. Silence.

Kenji's foot slides imperceptibly forward.

**SCENE 2**

**INT. DOJO - CONTINUOUS**

INSTANT EXPLOSION OF MOTION.

Kenji launches forward, a kinetic blur of speed lines.

Ryu parries. The two SHINAIS collide with a visual SHOCKWAVE effect—a burst of blue energy instead of sound.

A flurry of strikes. The animation frames skip to emphasize raw speed.

Close up on Kenji’s eyes, wide, pupils dilated.

Ryu spins, his SHINAI a sweeping arc leaving a trail of light.

Kenji ducks under the strike, bringing his SHINAI up in a thrust.

Ryu deflects it barely. The friction between the two bamboo swords creates sparks.

They lock swords, pressing with all their might. The bamboo bows under the pressure. Their faces are inches apart behind the protective grills, teeth gritted in extreme effort.

**SCENE 3**

**EXT. ABSTRACT VOID - CONTINUOUS (MENTAL SPACE)**

The dojo walls dissolve away. The background is now a swirling void of brushstrokes and abstract cherry blossom petals moving at high speed.

Kenji and Ryu are alone in this infinite space, still locked in their duel. The colors are hyper-saturated.

They break the lock and engage again. The movements are impossibly fast now, almost teleporting between strikes.

Every clash of the SHINAIS sends ripples through the painted void background.

Ryu leaps high into the "air," silhouetted against a massive, stylized sun. He brings his SHINAI down in a crushing overhead strike.

Kenji raises his SHINAI in a desperate horizontal block.

The impact is blinding. The screen flashes white.

**SCENE 4**

**INT. DOJO - LATER AFTERNOON**

Snap back to reality. The golden hour light is darker now.

Ryu stands tall, breathing heavily, his SHINAI held loosely at his side.

Kenji is on one knee on the floor, propping himself up with his SHINAI. He is winded but uninjured.

Ryu extends a hand.

Kenji looks up, takes a breath, and grips Ryu's hand.

Ryu pulls Kenji to his feet.

They both bow deeply to each other, a sign of absolute respect.

They turn and walk away side-by-side towards the exit, their SHINAIS resting on their shoulders against the setting sun.

**FADE OUT.**
