**Title: "A Ninja's Promise"**

**Logline:**
A young ninja faces a crucial test of trust and loyalty, proving that even the smallest acts of kindness can have a lasting impact.

---

**Scene 1: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*A busy afternoon in the village square. A few people walk around, engaged in their tasks. The sun casts a warm glow over the village.*

**NARRATOR (V.O.)**
In a world filled with duty, there are moments when friendship becomes the greatest strength.

*We see NARUTO (early 20s, blonde hair, wearing a headband) sitting on a bench, his face serious. He holds a small, crumpled piece of paper in his hand.*

---

**Scene 2: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*SASUKE (early 20s, dark hair, wearing a dark cloak) walks up, noticing Naruto on the bench. He pauses for a moment before sitting beside him.*

**SASUKE**
(looking at Naruto)
What’s bothering you?

**NARUTO**
(staring at the paper)
It’s a letter from Sakura... She’s gone on a mission. I promised her I’d keep an eye on things here. But what if something happens to her?

*Sasuke looks at Naruto, considering his words.*

---

**Scene 3: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*Naruto suddenly jumps up, clenching the paper in his fist.*

**NARUTO**
(sighing)
I’ve been a ninja for years... but some promises feel harder to keep than others.

*Sasuke stands up as well, his eyes focused on Naruto’s agitation.*

**SASUKE**
You think Sakura doesn’t trust you?

**NARUTO**
(suddenly looking up)
No! That’s not it... I trust her. But I... I don’t want to let her down.

---

**Scene 4: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*Sasuke takes a deep breath and then speaks with a rare hint of sincerity.*

**SASUKE**
You know, a promise isn’t just about keeping watch. It’s about showing up, no matter what. If she trusted you, she’s already certain you’ll be there when it matters.

*Naruto looks at Sasuke, his expression softening.*

**NARUTO**
(nods slowly)
Yeah... I guess you’re right. I just need to keep going.

---

**Scene 5: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*Naruto walks back toward the bench, sitting down again. Sasuke stays standing, watching him for a moment before looking away.*

**NARUTO**
(smiling)
Thanks, Sasuke. You know, you’re not so bad for someone who doesn’t talk much.

*Sasuke’s eyes soften, just a little, as he turns to leave.*

**SASUKE**
(smiling faintly)
Don’t let your guard down too much. She’ll probably be back before you know it.

---

**Scene 6: EXT. VILLAGE OUTSKIRTS - DAY**

*Sakura, wearing her ninja gear, appears on the horizon, walking toward the village. The familiar pink hair blowing in the wind.*

---

**Scene 7: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*Naruto stands up quickly, his face lighting up with a grin. He watches as Sakura walks into the village.*

**NARUTO**
(quietly to himself)
I kept my promise.

---

**Scene 8: EXT. VILLAGE SQUARE - DAY**

*As Sakura approaches, Naruto waves to her, his expression full of determination and warmth.*

**NARUTO**
Hey, Sakura! Welcome back!

*Sakura smiles warmly at him, relieved to see that everything is well. They exchange a brief, knowing look as the sun sets behind them.*

---

**THE END**
