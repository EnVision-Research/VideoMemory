**TITLE: NEON VELOCITY**

**GENRE: Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi 2D Animation**

**LOGLINE:** A courier must outrun a relentless enforcer across a vertical futuristic city using extreme parkour and a high-tech artifact.

**KEY PROP:** A glowing hexagonal **DATA CORE**.

***

**SCENE 1**

**EXT. NEO-TOKYO ROOFTOP - NIGHT**

Animation style: High contrast, neon colors (cyan and magenta), heavy use of glitch effects and perspective distortion.

Rain pours upwards—anti-gravity failure.

A sleek, glowing **DATA CORE (PROP)** spins in the air. A gloved hand snatches it.

KAI (20s, lithe, wearing a hooded tech-poncho) lands in a crouch. He secures the **DATA CORE** to his belt.

The rooftop access door blasts open.

VECTOR (20s, hulking, cybernetic armor, glowing visor) stomps out. He locks onto Kai.

Vector lunges. Kai backflips off the edge of the skyscraper, diving into the neon abyss.

**SCENE 2**

**EXT. VERTICAL ALLEYWAY - CONTINUOUS**

Kai free-falls past holographic advertisements. The camera follows him in a tight, shaky close-up.

He fires a grapple line. It snaps tight. He swings, wall-running horizontally along the side of a building.

Vector plummets past him, using magnetic boots to slam onto the wall. He runs vertically down the building, defying gravity.

Vector throws an energy discus.

Kai unclips the **DATA CORE** and holds it up. The Core emits a pulse shield. The energy discus deflects off the **DATA CORE** with a burst of pixelated sparks.

**SCENE 3**

**EXT. SUSPENSION CABLES - CONTINUOUS**

Kai vaults off the building and lands on massive steel cables connecting two towers. He balances effortlessly, the **DATA CORE** glowing brighter in his hand.

Vector lands heavily behind him, shaking the cables.

They sprint along the tightrope.

Vector swipes with a massive mechanical fist. Kai slides underneath, using the momentum to kick Vector away.

Kai tosses the **DATA CORE** into the air, performs a corkscrew flip over Vector’s head, and catches the **DATA CORE** on the landing.

**SCENE 4**

**EXT. LANDING PLATFORM - CONTINUOUS**

Kai lands on a flat platform. A hover-drone is approaching in the distance.

Vector crashes down between Kai and the drone. He blocks the path.

Vector charges, preparing a final tackle.

Kai doesn't run. He waits.

At the last second, Kai slams the **DATA CORE** into a slot on the floor.

A blast of blue energy erupts from the floor panels. A jump-pad activates.

Kai is launched high into the air, over Vector’s head.

**SCENE 5**

**EXT. SKY - CONTINUOUS**

Slow motion.

Kai floats in the air, silhouetted against a massive holographic moon. He clutches the **DATA CORE** to his chest, safe.

Below, Vector watches, defeated, as the distance grows.

Kai grabs the skid of the passing hover-drone and speeds away into the neon skyline.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
