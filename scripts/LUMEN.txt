**TITLE: “LUMEN”**
*(3D Animated Short | ~64 seconds | Three-Act | ~8 shots)*

---

## **ACT 1**

**1. EXT. ROOFTOP GARDEN – DAWN**

Soft sunrise. Stylized city skyline. MAYA (27) stands among dew-bright plants, holding a **GLOWING LUMEN ORB** the size of a grapefruit. The orb’s light pulses with her breath.

MAYA studies the orb—hopeful, nervous.

**MAYA**
(whisper)
Okay… one last try.

She turns the orb. A tiny constellation flickers inside.

---

**2. INT. GLASS ELEVATOR – MORNING**

The elevator glides upward. MAYA’s reflection multiplies in the glass. The **LUMEN ORB** floats just above her palm, obedient like a trained firefly.

A chime. The orb briefly dims—like it’s listening.

**MAYA**
You remember him, don’t you?

The orb answers with a gentle shimmer.

---

**3. INT. HOLOGRAM WORKSHOP – MORNING**

A clean, futuristic studio. Tools hover on magnetic arms. ELI (32) waits by a worktable—calm eyes, kind posture.

MAYA enters. She sets the **LUMEN ORB** between them. It projects a soft, swirling hologram—abstract memories, no faces.

ELI smiles, bittersweet.

**ELI**
It still lights up for you.

**MAYA**
It’s not for me.
It’s for… closure.

---

## **ACT 2**

**4. EXT. SUBWAY PLATFORM – DAY**

Crowds blur in stylized motion trails. MAYA stands alone near the edge line (safe distance). The station lights stutter in rhythmic patterns.

She checks her empty hand—no orb. Just her pulse.

Her phone buzzes: *“If you’re sure, meet me.”*
She inhales, steadies.

---

**5. INT. ART GALLERY – AFTERNOON**

White walls. Floating frames rotate slowly. ELI and MAYA face each other beside a minimalist sculpture.

ELI holds the **LUMEN ORB** now. The orb’s glow is uneven—half warm, half cool.

**ELI**
You asked me to keep it…
because you couldn’t.

**MAYA**
Because I kept carrying the same version of us.

The orb projects two light-figures that almost touch—then drift apart.

---

**6. EXT. NEON ALLEY – EVENING**

Rainy pavement reflects neon signs. ELI stands under an awning, alone. The **LUMEN ORB** hovers near his chest, rain beading off an invisible field around it.

He turns it gently—like turning a page.

**ELI**
(to the orb)
Don’t trap her in yesterday.

The orb steadies—glow becoming clear and balanced.

---

## **ACT 3**

**7. EXT. SEASIDE CLIFF – SUNSET**

Waves far below. Wind tugs at MAYA’s coat. ELI approaches, careful, respectful distance. The **LUMEN ORB** floats between them, casting sunset colors onto their faces.

MAYA reaches out. ELI doesn’t stop her.

Her fingers touch the orb—its light becomes warm and bright, steady for the first time.

**MAYA**
Thank you… for not chasing me.

**ELI**
Thank you… for choosing yourself.

A quiet beat. The orb rises slightly, as if relieved.

---

**8. INT. OBSERVATORY DOME – NIGHT**

A massive telescope. Star maps glow on the floor. MAYA stands alone beneath the open dome. The **LUMEN ORB** circles her slowly, projecting constellations that align with the real sky.

MAYA smiles—small, genuine.

**MAYA**
(to the orb)
New story. Same light.

She opens her hand. The orb floats upward, joining the stars—its glow blending into the night like a final punctuation.

**FADE OUT.**
